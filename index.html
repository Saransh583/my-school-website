About Our School

Our Mission

Victor Convent Higher Secondary School is dedicated to nurturing young minds through holistic education that combines academic excellence with moral values and community service.

Our History

Founded in 1990 by the Victor Education Society, our school has grown from a modest institution to one of the most respected educational establishments in the region.

With over 30 years of educational excellence, we've consistently ranked among the top schools in board examinations while maintaining a 100% placement record for college admissions.

Our alumni network includes professionals across industries, many of whom return to mentor current students and contribute to the school's development.

Academic Programs

Primary Education

Our primary program focuses on building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and creative thinking through interactive learning methods.

  • CBSE curriculum with Montessori influences
  • Focus on experiential learning
  • Individual attention with low student-teacher ratio

Special Features

Phonics Program Abacus Training Storytelling Gardening

Secondary Education

Grades 6-10 follow a rigorous academic curriculum while encouraging exploration of individual interests through electives.

  • CBSE curriculum
